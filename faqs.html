FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

About Our Surf Lessons

What services are included in our surf lesson package?

Our package features hands-on instruction from experienced coaches, a mix of theory and practical sessions in the water, full equipment rental (surfboard, wetsuit, leash), and thorough safety guidance throughout your lesson.

How long is each surf lesson session?

Each session typically lasts between 1 to 2 hours, adjusted to ensure you receive optimal coaching based on the current wave conditions.

What is the schedule for your surf lessons?

Our lessons are available in the early morning and late afternoon when the waves are at their best. For the most up-to-date schedule, please contact us directly via WhatsApp.

Equipment and Preparation

What equipment is provided during the lessons?

We supply all the essential gear—including surfboards, wetsuits (if needed), leashes, and other safety equipment—ensuring everything is in top condition for your lesson.

Do I need to bring any personal gear?

We recommend bringing your own swimwear, towel, and sunscreen. While we provide the main equipment, your personal items will make your experience more comfortable.

What should I prepare before joining a surf lesson?

We suggest doing some light stretching or warm-up exercises beforehand, bringing your personal items, and ensuring you're in good physical condition. A brief safety orientation will be provided at the start of each session.

Safety and Experience

How do your instructors ensure safety during the lessons?

Our instructors are professionally trained to prioritize your safety. They provide detailed briefings, closely monitor conditions, and are ready to respond in any situation. For further details, please reach out to our team via WhatsApp.

Is limited swimming ability a concern?

Not at all. Our program is designed for all levels, including those with limited swimming skills. Our instructors provide extra support and tailor techniques to meet your needs.

Additional Information

When is the best time of year to join a surf lesson in Bali?

The ideal time is generally between April and October when the waves and weather conditions are optimal. However, conditions can vary, so it's a good idea to check the latest forecasts before booking.

Do you have any tips for maximizing my surf learning experience?

Arrive well-rested, perform a proper warm-up, and follow your instructor's guidance closely. Consistent practice and direct communication with our team via WhatsApp will help you get the most out of your lesson.
