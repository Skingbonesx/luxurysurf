Our Location

How to Find Us

We're conveniently located along Kuta Beach's one-way street. The beach will be on your left side as you drive, and you'll find several notable landmarks on your right that will help guide you to our location.

Step-by-Step Directions

First Landmark: Beachwalk Shopping Center

Once you spot the Beachwalk Shopping Center on your right, you're getting close! Continue driving for approximately 550 meters.

Second Landmark: Tribe Bali Kuta Beach

Look for Tribe Bali on your right. This modern building is a key landmark.

Beach Entrance Gate

Directly across from Tribe Bali, you'll see the distinctive beach entrance gate on your left.

Final Landmark: Lifeguard Post

A short distance from the gate, you'll see the lifeguard post. We're located right in front of this post!

You've Arrived!

Welcome to Luxury Surf Bali! Our friendly staff will be ready to greet you and start your surfing adventure.

Additional Tips

  • Parking is available nearby
  • We're just a short walk from the beach entrance
  • Look for our banner and flags on the beach
  • Feel free to contact us if you need help finding us
