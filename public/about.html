About Luxury Surf Bali

At Luxury Surf Bali, the ocean is our playground and every wave tells its own story. Founded in 2020 with a passion for sharing the magic of surfing, we are a small, dedicated team committed to transforming your first paddle into an unforgettable adventure.

Nestled near the iconic Kuta Beach, our surf lessons go beyond just catching waves. They’re an invitation to immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Bali, where each lesson is a blend of personalized coaching, safety-first practices, and a deep respect for the sea.

Our instructors are more than just surfing experts—they’re passionate storytellers who infuse every session with the spirit of Bali’s rich surf heritage. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, we tailor our lessons to match your pace, ensuring that you not only learn but also fall in love with the ocean.

Every session starts with a warm welcome, a comprehensive safety briefing, and a shared commitment to respecting nature. We believe that the journey to mastering the waves is as much about understanding the ocean as it is about learning to ride it.

So, come join us at Luxury Surf Bali—feel the sand beneath your feet, let the rhythm of the waves guide you, and discover the beauty of a life lived close to the sea.

Welcome to our surf family, where your adventure begins and every wave is a new opportunity.
