What's in Store for You at Luxury Surf Bali?

Ride the waves, make new friends, and experience the true spirit of Kuta Beach

At Luxury Surf Bali, every day brings a new adventure on the waves. Whether you're stepping into the surf for the first time or you're a seasoned rider looking to sharpen your skills, our tailored surf lessons and top-quality board rentals are designed just for you.

Our friendly, expert instructors are here to guide you through every twist and turn of the ocean, ensuring that you not only learn but also have a blast along the way. With our service just a short stroll from the world-famous Kuta Beach, you're always steps away from catching the perfect wave.

Beginner Lesson Steps

Lesson 1 - Safety Basics

Begin your surfing journey with essential safety knowledge. Learn about ocean awareness, surfing etiquette, and basic water safety protocols. Our experienced instructors will guide you through proper equipment handling and beach safety guidelines, ensuring you're well-prepared for your surfing adventure.

Lesson 2 - Pop-up and Stance

Learn the fundamental pop-up technique and proper surfing stance. Practice the explosive movement that takes you from lying on the board to standing up, and understand the importance of foot positioning and weight distribution. Master these basics on land before taking them to the water.

Lesson 3 - Riding and Maneuvering

Take your surfing to the next level by learning basic maneuvers and riding techniques. Practice turning, trimming along the wave face, and maintaining speed. Our experienced instructors will guide you through progressive exercises to improve your wave riding skills and build confidence in the water.
